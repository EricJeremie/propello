About Propello

We help experts move faster without losing judgment.

Propello was built for teams that need to move from scattered client context to a clear commercial document — without losing nuance, taste, or brand quality.

Our point of view

AI should support the expert, not replace the expert.

The best proposals still need human taste: what to include, what to exclude, how to price, and how to communicate risk. Propello handles the structure so teams can focus on decisions.

What we optimize for

Clarity before cleverness.

We prioritize clean scopes, grounded assumptions, sensible terms, and proposal language a client can understand quickly.

How it grows

Industry profiles, not one-size-fits-all templates.

Propello adapts templates, questions, deliverables, exclusions, and pricing guidance to different service industries.

Operating principles

Small product, strong habits.

Grounded outputsUse the facts provided and mark uncertainty instead of inventing details.
Editable systemsKeep templates and industry content structured so teams can improve them over time.
Quiet confidenceWrite proposals that feel polished, direct, and commercially mature.
