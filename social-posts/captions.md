**Use with:** `output/01-founder-build-post.png` to `output/06-landing-page-macbook.png`

I've been building Propello because proposals are one of those parts of client work that can quietly slow everything down. 🧩

You already talked to the client. You already understand the project. But then you still need to turn messy notes, PDF briefs, scope decisions, pricing, timelines, terms, and requirements into something clear enough to send.

Propello is my attempt to make that workflow faster with AI. ⚡

It helps service teams move from brief to client-ready draft by combining:

- 🤖 AI proposal generation from briefs, RFPs, and internal notes
- 🧠 AI requirements assistant for clarifying what the client actually needs
- 🔗 Guided questionnaire and client invite links
- ✍️ Editable proposal preview before sending
- 📄 Branded PDF export
- 🧱 Templates, reusable scope blocks, and industry profiles
- 💸 Pricing packages, line items, milestones, and payment terms
- ✅ Review status, approval notes, and version snapshots
- 🗂️ Invoices, dashboard/history, quick search, and find-in-proposal
- 🤝 Sharing, real-time collaboration, and Supabase-backed accounts

The landing page is also coming together around the same promise: AI-assisted proposals for real service teams that need to scope, price, review, and ship client work faster. 🚀

Propello is still in beta testing, and I'm planning to launch it very soon. I would love feedback from builders, agencies, consultants, freelancers, and service teams here in App Builders PH.
